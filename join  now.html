Environmental Facts You Should Know

Over 1 million plastic bottles are bought every minute worldwide.

One tree absorbs 22 kg of CO₂ per year and provides oxygen for two people.

Globally, 1 in 3 people lack access to safe drinking water.

Over 100,000 marine animals die annually due to plastic pollution.

Food waste generates about 8–10% of global greenhouse gas emissions.

Switching to LEDs can cut lighting energy use by 50–70%.

City trees can reduce neighborhood temperatures by up to 2°C.

Reusable bottles can save 1000+ single-use bottles per person over time.

Our Mission & Future Goals

EcoPath exists to build everyday eco‑habits that last—through education, community action, and youth leadership.

Mission Focus

  • Reduce single‑use plastics via refill habits
  • Grow urban tree canopy and tree care
  • Promote composting and waste segregation
  • Enable student‑led climate initiatives

Goals 2026–2030

  • Set up more refill stations in campuses
  • Annual inter‑school eco‑challenges
  • Neighborhood composting in every district
  • Train 1,000+ youth eco‑ambassadors

FAQs
